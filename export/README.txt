The game has two controlschemes and can switch between left and right hand controls.
Press A or D-Pad Down to switch handedness.
Press B or D-Pad Right to switch controlschemes.

Flying scheme controls:
Stick - Look around
Shoulder button - move backwards
Shoulder trigger - move forwards

Stick scheme controls:
Shoulder button switches between movement mode and lookaround mode.
Stick left and right turn left and right.
Stick up and down either move forward and backward or look up and down depending on mode.

Also parts of the model start to grow in Stick lookaround mode.
